## Tech Stack!


: HTML/CSS , React , Redux , MockBee

### Features

- Create and Login with your own credentials
- Like and Comment on your faviorite posts
- Share Your thoughts
- Edit / Modify Posts
- Create your Profile
- Edit your Profile
- Follow Unfollow users
- BookMarks your Faviorite posts
